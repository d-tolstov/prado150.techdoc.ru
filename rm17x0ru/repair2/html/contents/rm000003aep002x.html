1. CHECK TRANSMISSION OIL



  1. Park the vehicle on a level surface.

  1. Remove the transmission filler plug and gasket.

  1. Check that the oil level is within 5 mm (0.196 in.) of the lowest point of the transmission filler plug opening.
    If the result is not as specified, add transmission oil.

    Viscosity:
    SAE 75W

    Standard Capacity:
    2.1 liters (2.2 US qts, 1.8 Imp. qts)

    ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
    1. Too much or too little oil will lead to transmission problems.
    2. After adjusting the oil level, drive the vehicle and check the oil level again.

  1. Inspect for oil leaks when the oil level is low.

  1. Install a new gasket and the transmission filler plug.

    Момент затяжки:
    37 N*m{ 377 kgf*cm , 27 ft.*lbf }
