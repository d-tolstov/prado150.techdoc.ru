1.WHEN USING INTELLIGENT TESTER

ПРЕДОСТЕРЕЖЕНИЕ:
  1. Observe the following items for safety reasons:
  1. Read the tester's instructions prior to use.
  2. Make sure that the tester cable is not caught on the pedals, shift lever or steering wheel when driving with the tester connected to the vehicle.
  3. When driving the vehicle with the tester connected, make sure 2 people are present. One person should drive the vehicle and the other should operate the tester.


2.WHEN USING BATTERY DURING INSPECTION
  1. While using the battery during inspections, do not bring the positive and negative tester probes too close to each other as a short circuit may occur.
