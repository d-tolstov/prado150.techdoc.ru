1. REMOVE MANUAL TRANSMISSION ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the manual transmission assembly (See page Нажмите здесь).



2. REMOVE CLUTCH RELEASE FORK SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the release fork and release bearing from the clutch housing.



3. REMOVE CLUTCH RELEASE BEARING ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the clip and release bearing from the release fork.



4. REMOVE CLUTCH RELEASE FORK BOOT


5. REMOVE RELEASE FORK SUPPORT

  1. Remove the release fork support from the manual transmission unit.



6. REMOVE CLUTCH COVER ASSEMBLY



  1. Place matchmarks on the clutch cover and flywheel.
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Matchmark

  1. Loosen each set bolt one turn at a time until spring tension is released.

  1. Remove the 6 set bolts and pull off the clutch cover.

    ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
    Do not drop the clutch disc.



7. REMOVE CLUTCH DISC ASSEMBLY

ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
Keep the lining part of the clutch disc, the pressure plate and the surface of the flywheel away from oil and foreign matter.
