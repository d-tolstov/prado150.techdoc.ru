1. INSTALL REAR CRANKSHAFT OIL SEAL



  1. Using SST and a hammer, tap in a new oil seal until its surface is flush with the oil seal retainer edge.

    SST
    09223-15030  
    09950-70010   (09951-07100)

  1. Apply MP grease to lip of the oil seal.



2. INSTALL REAR CRANKSHAFT OIL SEAL RETAINER

  1. Install a new gasket and the retainer with the 4 bolts.

    Момент затяжки:
    13 N*m{ 133 kgf*cm , 10 ft.*lbf }



3. INSTALL FRONT CRANKSHAFT OIL SEAL



  1. Using SST and a hammer, tap in a new oil seal until its surface is flush with the timing belt case edge.

    SST
    09214-60010  

  1. Apply MP grease to the lip of the oil seal.



4. INSTALL TIMING BELT CASE SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Place a new gasket on the cylinder block.

  1. Install the timing belt case with the 5 bolts.

    Момент затяжки:

5. INSTALL OIL STRAINER SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Install a new gasket and the oil strainer with the 2 bolts and 2 nuts.

6. INSTALL OIL PAN SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove any old packing (FIPG) material and do not drop any oil on the contact surfaces of the oil pan and cylinder block.

    1. Using a gasket scraper, remove all the old packing (FIPG) material from the installation surface.
    2. Thoroughly clean all components to remove all the loose material.
    3. Using a non-residue solvent, clean both of the sealing surfaces.

      ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
      Do not use a solvent which will affect the painted surfaces.




  1. Apply seal packing to the oil pan as shown in the illustration.

    Seal packing:
    Toyota Genuine Seal Packing Black, Three Bond 1207B or equivalent

    Application Specification:
    Item
    		Seal Packing Diameter
    		Seal Packing Application Length
    Dashed line
    		7.0 mm (0.276 in.)
    		128 mm (5.04 in.)
    Continuous line
    		5.0 mm (0.197 in.)
    		-

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Seal Packing
    *a
    		Timing Belt Case Contact Portion
    *b
    		Rear Oil Seal Retainer Contact Portion

    УКАЗАНИЕ:
    1. Do not apply an excessive amount to the surface, especially near the oil passages.
    2. Parts must be assembled within 5 minutes of application. Otherwise the material must be removed and reapplied.

  1. Install the oil pan with the 16 bolts and 2 nuts. Uniformly tighten the bolts and nuts in several steps.

    Момент затяжки:
    18 N*m{ 184 kgf*cm , 13 ft.*lbf }

11. INSTALL CYLINDER HEAD GASKET


  1. Check the piston protrusion for each cylinder.




    1. Find where the piston head protrudes most by slowly turning the crankshaft clockwise and counterclockwise.
      Text in Illustration 
      *a
      		Measuring Tip
      *b
      		Protrusion




    2. Measure the protrusion of each cylinder at 2 places as shown in the illustration, making a total of 8 measurements.
      Text in Illustration 
      *a
      		Measuring Point

      		Front

    3. For the piston protrusion value of each cylinder, use the average of the 2 measurements of that cylinder.

      Standard protrusion:
      0.68 to 0.98 mm (0.0268 to 0.0386 in.)

      After installing the piston and connecting rod assembly, if the protrusion is not as specified, remove the piston and connecting rod assembly and reinstall them.




  1. Select a new cylinder head gasket.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Cutout Mark

    		Front

    УКАЗАНИЕ:
    There are 3 sizes of new cylinder head gaskets, marked "B", "D" or "F" accordingly.

    New Cylinder Head Gasket Thickness:
    Item
    		Specified Condition
    Mark B
    		1.40 to 1.50 mm (0.0551 to 0.0591 in.)
    Mark D
    		1.50 to 1.60 mm (0.0591 to 0.0630 in.)
    Mark F
    		1.60 to 1.70 mm (0.0630 to 0.0669 in.)


    1. Select the largest piston protrusion value from the measurements made, then select a new appropriate gasket according to the table below.

      Gasket Size:
      Piston Protrusion
      		Gasket Size
      0.68 to 0.78 mm (0.0268 to 0.0307 in.)
      		Use B
      0.78 to 0.88 mm (0.0307 to 0.0346 in.)
      		Use D
      0.88 to 0.98 mm (0.0346 to 0.0385 in.)
      		Use F




  1. Install the selected cylinder head gasket to the cylinder block.

    ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
    Make sure that the gasket is installed facing the proper direction.
    Be careful of the installation direction.



12. INSTALL CYLINDER HEAD SUB-ASSEMBLY

УКАЗАНИЕ:
  1. The cylinder head bolts are tightened in 3 progressive steps.
  2. Set the No. 1 cylinder to 90° BTDC/compression to avoid interference with the piston top and valve head.
  3. If any bolt is broken or deformed, replace it.




  1. Using the crankshaft pulley bolt, turn the crankshaft 90° counterclockwise, and align the timing mark of the crankshaft timing pulley with the protrusion of the timing belt case.
    Text in Illustration 

    		Turn

  1. Place the cylinder head on the cylinder block.

    ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
    1. Make sure that no oil is on the mounting surface of the cylinder head.
    2. Place the cylinder head on the cylinder block gently in order not to damage the gasket with the bottom part of the head.

  1. Install the plate washers to the cylinder head bolts.

  1. Apply a light coat of engine oil to the threads and under the heads of the cylinder head bolts.



  1. Step 1:




    1. Install and uniformly tighten the 18 cylinder head bolts, in several steps, in the sequence shown in the illustrations.

      Момент затяжки:
      78 N*m{ 795 kgf*cm , 58 ft.*lbf }

      УКАЗАНИЕ:
      Each bolt length is indicated below.

      Standard Bolt:
      Item
      		Length
      Bolt A
      		107 mm (4.21 in.)
      Bolt B
      		127 mm (5.00 in.)

      If any one of the cylinder head bolts does not meet the torque specification, replace the cylinder set head bolt.

  1. Step 2:

    1. Mark the front side of each cylinder head bolt head with paint.

    2. Tighten the cylinder head bolts 90° in the sequence shown in step 1.

  1. Step 3:

    1. Tighten the cylinder head bolts another 90° in the sequence shown in step 1.

  1. Check that the paint marks are now at a 180° angle to the front.
