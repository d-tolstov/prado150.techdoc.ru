Turn the ignition switch off for 60 seconds before performing this inspection.




  1. Turn the ignition switch to the ON position, measure the time when the battery voltage is applied to the glow plugs.

4. INSPECT GLOW PLUGS

ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
Turn the ignition switch off for 60 seconds before performing this inspection.




  1. Using an ohmmeter, measure the resistance between the glow plug terminal and ground.

    Standard resistance:
    Approx. 0.72Ωat 20°C (68°F)

    ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
    1. Do not damage the glow plug pipes as it could cause an open circuit or shorten life of the glow plugs.
    2. Avoid getting oil and gasoline on the glow plug when cleaning.
    3. During inspection, be sure to wipe any oil on the terminal and bakelite washer with a dry cloth.
    4. Do not apply more than 11 V to the glow plug as it could cause an open circuit.
