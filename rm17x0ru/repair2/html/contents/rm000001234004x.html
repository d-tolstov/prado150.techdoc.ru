1. INSTALL FRONT CRANKSHAFT OIL SEAL



  1. Apply MP grease to the lip of a new oil seal.

  1. Using SST and a hammer, tap in the oil seal to a depth of 0.5 mm (0.0197 in.) from the oil pump case edge.

    SST
    09214-60010  



2. INSTALL CRANKSHAFT TIMING PULLEY



  1. Align the key groove of the timing pulley with the pulley set key.

  1. Using SST and a hammer, tap in the timing pulley with the flange side facing inward.

    SST
    09223-46011  
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Inside
