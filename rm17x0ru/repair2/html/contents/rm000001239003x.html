1. REMOVE MANUAL TRANSMISSION ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the manual transmission (See page Нажмите здесь).



2. REMOVE CLUTCH COVER ASSEMBLY



  1. Place matchmarks on the clutch cover and flywheel.
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Matchmark

  1. Loosen each set bolt one turn at a time until spring tension is released.

  1. Remove the 6 set bolts and pull off the clutch cover.

    ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
    Do not drop the clutch disc.



3. REMOVE CLUTCH DISC ASSEMBLY

ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
Keep the lining part of the clutch disc, the pressure plate and the surface of the flywheel away from oil and foreign matter.



4. REMOVE FLYWHEEL SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Using SST, hold the crankshaft.

    SST
    09213-54015   (91651-60855)
    09330-00021  

  1. Remove the 8 bolts and flywheel.



5. REMOVE REAR END PLATE

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and rear end plate.



6. REMOVE FLYWHEEL HOUSING DUST SEAL


7. REMOVE REAR CRANKSHAFT OIL SEAL



  1. Using a knife, cut off the lip of the oil seal.
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Cut Position

  1. Using a screwdriver, pry out the oil seal.

    ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
    After the removal, check that the crankshaft is not damaged. If damaged, repair it with a sandpaper (#400).

    УКАЗАНИЕ:
    Tape the screwdriver before use.
