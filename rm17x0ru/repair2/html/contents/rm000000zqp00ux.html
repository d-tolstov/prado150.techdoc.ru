DESCRIPTION

When the ignition switch is turned to ON, the battery voltage is applied to terminal IGSW of the ECM. The ECM "MREL" output signal causes current to flow to the EFI relay coil, closing the contacts of the EFI relay and supplying power to terminal +B of the ECM.
