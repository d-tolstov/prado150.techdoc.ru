Waveform 1



  1. A/C amplifier signal
    Terminal No. (Symbol)
    		G61-8 (THWO) - C101-14 (E1)
    Tester Range
    		5 V/DIV., 0.1 sec./DIV.
    Condition
    		Idling

    УКАЗАНИЕ:
    Waveform time A changes according to coolant temperature.


Waveform 2



  1. Speed signal
    Terminal No. (Symbol)
    		G61-9 (SPD) - C101-14 (E1)
    Tester Range
    		2 V/DIV., 20 msec./DIV.
    Condition
    		Driving at approx. 20 km/h (12.4 mph)

    УКАЗАНИЕ:
    1. As vehicle speed increases, the wavelength becomes shorter.
    2. As vehicle speed increases, the waveform's amplitude becomes greater.


Waveform 3



  1. Engine speed signal
    Terminal No. (Symbol)
    		G62-7 (TACH) - C101-14 (E1)
    Tester Range
    		5 V/DIV., 20 msec./DIV.
    Condition
    		Idling after warming up

    УКАЗАНИЕ:
    As engine speed increases, the wavelength becomes shorter.
