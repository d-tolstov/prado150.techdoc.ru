1. MOUNT INJECTION PUMP



  1. Mount the injection pump on SST.

    SST
    09241-76022  
    09245-54010  



2. REMOVE NO. 4 NOZZLE LEAKAGE PIPE



  1. Remove the overflow screw, nozzle leakage pipe and 2 washers.

  1. Remove the bolt and wiring bracket.



3. REMOVE FUEL FILTER TO INJECTION PUMP FUEL PIPE

  1. Remove the cap nut, fuel pipe and 2 washers.

  1. Remove the fuel inlet hollow screw and washer.



4. REMOVE TIMING CONTROL VALVE



  1. Using a 5 mm hexagon wrench, remove the 2 bolts and timing control valve.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		5 mm Hexagon Wrench



5. REMOVE DISTRIBUTIVE HEAD PLUG



  1. Using SST, remove the distributive head plug.

    SST
    09260-54012   (09262-54010)

  1. Remove the O-ring.



6. REMOVE DELIVERY VALVE



  1. Using SST, remove the 4 delivery valve holders and 4 springs.

    SST
    09260-54012   (09269-54020)

  1. Remove the 4 delivery valves and 4 gaskets.

    УКАЗАНИЕ:
    Arrange the delivery valves, springs and holders in the order they were removed.

    ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
    Do not touch the sliding surfaces of the delivery valve with your hand.
