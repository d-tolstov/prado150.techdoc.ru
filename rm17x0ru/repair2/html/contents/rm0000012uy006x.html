1. INSPECT DIESEL THROTTLE BODY


  1. Inspect the throttle control motor.




    1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

      Standard Resistance:
      Tester Connection
      		Condition
      		Specified Condition
      1 - 2
      		20°C (68°F)
      		18 to 22 Ω
      2 - 3
      		20°C (68°F)
      		18 to 22 Ω
      4 - 5
      		20°C (68°F)
      		18 to 22 Ω
      5 - 6
      		20°C (68°F)
      		18 to 22 Ω

      Text in Illustration 
      *a
      		Component without harness connected
      (Diesel Throttle Body)
      If the result is not as specified, replace the diesel throttle body.



  1. Inspect the throttle open switch.




    1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

      Standard Resistance:
      Tester Connection
      		Condition
      		Specified Condition
      1 - 2
      		Fully open
      		Below 1 Ω
      1 - 2
      		Fully close
      		10 kΩ or higher

      Text in Illustration 
      *a
      		Component without harness connected
      (Diesel Throttle Body)
      If the result is not as specified, replace the diesel throttle body.
