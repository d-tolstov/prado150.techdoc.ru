1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
  1. After turning the ignition switch off, waiting time may be required before disconnecting the cable from the battery terminal. Therefore, make sure to read the disconnecting the cable from the battery terminal notice before proceeding with work (See page Нажмите здесь).
  2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (See page Нажмите здесь).

16. REMOVE FUEL TANK MAIN TUBE SUB-ASSEMBLY AND FUEL RETURN TUBE SUB-ASSEMBLY (for Single Tank Type)



  1. Remove the 2 fuel tube joint clips and pull out the fuel tank main tube and fuel return tube.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Fuel Tube
    *2
    		Fuel Tube Joint
    *3
    		Fuel Tube Joint Clip
    *4
    		O-Ring

    ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
    1. Remove any dirt and foreign matter on the fuel tube joint before performing this work.
    2. Do not allow any scratches or foreign matter on the parts when disconnecting them, as the fuel tube joint contains the O-rings that seal the plug.
    3. Perform this work by hand. Do not use any tools.
    4. Do not forcibly bend, twist or turn the nylon tube.
    5. Protect the disconnected part by covering it with a plastic bag and tape after disconnecting the fuel tubes.




  1. Remove the fuel tank main tube and fuel return tube from the fuel tank.

18. REMOVE FUEL TANK VENT TUBE ASSEMBLY



  1. Set SST on the retainer.

    SST
    09808-14030  

    УКАЗАНИЕ:
    1. Engage the claws of SST securely with the retainer holes to secure SST.
    2. Install SST while pressing the claws of SST against the retainer (towards the center of SST).




  1. Using SST, loosen the retainer.

    SST
    09808-14030  
    Text in Illustration 

    		Turn

    УКАЗАНИЕ:
    Fit the tips of SST onto the ribs of the retainer.

    ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
    1. When the retainer is loosened, be careful as the fuel tank vent tube will spring upward from the force of the spring.
    2. Remove any foreign matter around the fuel tank vent tube before this operation.

  1. Remove the retainer.

  1. Remove the fuel tank vent tube assembly from the fuel tank.

    ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
    Be careful not to bend the arm of the fuel sender gauge.

  1. Remove the gasket from the fuel tank.

20. REMOVE FUEL TANK TO FILLER PIPE HOSE
