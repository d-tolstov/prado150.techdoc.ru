2. INSTALL NO. 1 TAPER SCREW PLUG

  1. Apply adhesive to 2 or 3 threads of the screw plug.

    Adhesive:
    Toyota Genuine Adhesive 1324, Three Bond 1324 or equivalent




  1. Install the screw plug.

    Момент затяжки:
    20 N*m{ 199 kgf*cm , 14 ft.*lbf }

    Adhesive:

    Text in Illustration

  1. Install the water drain cock.

    Момент затяжки:

    Text in Illustration

    УКАЗАНИЕ:
    1. After tightening the drain cock to the specified torque, rotate the drain union clockwise until its drain port is facing downward.

4. INSTALL CYLINDER BLOCK OIL ORIFICE

  1. Using a 6 mm hexagon socket wrench, install the oil orifice.

    Момент затяжки:
    11 N*m{ 110 kgf*cm , 8 ft.*lbf }



5. INSTALL NO. 1 OIL NOZZLE SUB-ASSEMBLY

ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
Use the same procedure for all oil nozzle No. 1 sub-assemblies.




  1. Align the pin of the oil nozzle with the one of the pin holes of the cylinder block.

  1. Install the oil nozzle with the check valve.

    Момент затяжки:
    26 N*m{ 260 kgf*cm , 19 ft.*lbf }



6. INSTALL PISTON SUB-ASSEMBLY


  1. Assemble the piston and connecting rod.




    1. Using snap ring pliers, install a new snap ring on one side of the piston pin hole.

    2. Gradually heat the piston to about 60°C (140°F).

    3. Coat the piston pin with engine oil.




    4. Align the front marks of the piston and connecting rod, connect the connecting rod to the piston, and then push in the piston pin with your thumb.
      Text in Illustration 
      *1
      		Front Mark

    5. Check the fitting condition between the piston and piston pin. Try to move the piston back and forth on the piston pin.




    6. Using snap ring pliers, install a new snap ring on the other side of the piston pin hole.

8. INSTALL CONNECTING ROD BEARING



  1. Align the bearing claw with the groove of the connecting rod or connecting rod cap.

  1. Install the bearings to the connecting rod and connecting rod cap.



9. INSTALL CRANKSHAFT BEARING

УКАЗАНИЕ:
Upper bearings have an oil groove and oil hole; lower bearings do not.




  1. Align the bearing claw with the claw groove of the cylinder block, and push in the 5 upper bearings to install them.




  1. Align the bearing claw with the claw groove of the crankshaft bearing cap, and push in the 5 lower bearings to install them.

11. INSTALL CRANKSHAFT

  1. Place the crankshaft on the cylinder block.




  1. Install the 5 crankshaft bearing caps in their proper locations.



  1. Install the crankshaft bearing cap bolts.

    1. Apply a light coat of the engine oil to the threads and under the bolt heads of the crankshaft bearing caps.




    2. Install and uniformly tighten the 10 bolts of the crankshaft bearing caps, in several steps, in the sequence shown in the illustration.

      Момент затяжки:
      105 N*m{ 1071 kgf*cm , 77 ft.*lbf }

  1. Check that the crankshaft turns smoothly.

  1. Check the crankshaft thrust clearance (See page Нажмите здесь).



12. INSTALL PISTON AND CONNECTING ROD



  1. Cover the connecting rod bolts with a short piece of hose to protect the crankshaft and cylinder bore from damage.




  1. Using a piston ring compressor, push the correctly numbered piston and connecting rod assembly into the cylinder with the front mark of the piston facing forward.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Front Mark



  1. Place the connecting rod cap on the connecting rod.

    1. Match each numbered connecting rod cap with the correct connecting rod.




    2. Install the connection rod cap with the front mark facing forward.
      Text in Illustration 
      *1
      		Front Mark

  1. Install the connecting rod cap nuts.

    ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
    If any connecting rod bolt is broken or deformed, replace it.

    УКАЗАНИЕ:
    The connecting rod cap nuts are tightened in 2 progressive steps.

  1. Apply a light of engine oil to the threads and under the heads of the connecting rod cap nuts.



  1. Step 1:




    1. Install and alternately tighten the nuts of the connecting rod cap in several passes.

      Момент затяжки:
      54 N*m{ 551 kgf*cm , 40 ft.*lbf }

      УКАЗАНИЕ:
      If any one of the connecting rod cap nuts does not meet the torque specification, replace them.

  1. Step 2:

    1. Mark the front side of the connecting rod cap nuts with paint.

    2. Tighten the connecting rod cap nuts 90° in the shown step 1.

  1. Check that the painted marks are now at a 90° angle to the front.

  1. Check that the crankshaft turns smoothly.

  1. Check the connecting rod thrust clearance (See page Нажмите здесь).
