1. REMOVE FUEL TANK VENT TUBE ASSEMBLY

  1. for Single Tank Type:
    Remove the fuel tank vent tube assembly (See page Нажмите здесь).

  1. for Double Tank Type:

    1. for Fuel Main Tank:
      Remove the fuel tank vent tube assembly (See page Нажмите здесь).

    2. for Fuel Sub Tank:
      Remove the fuel tank vent tube assembly (See page Нажмите здесь).



2. REMOVE FUEL SENDER GAUGE ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the fuel sender gauge connector.




  1. Press down on the fuel sender gauge claw labeled A. Then slide the fuel sender gauge upward to remove it.
    Text in Illustration 

    		Press down

    		Slide



3. REMOVE FUEL SENDER GAUGE ASSEMBLY (for Fuel Sub Tank)



  1. Remove the 2 screws.




  1. Press down on the fuel sender gauge claw labeled A. Then slide the fuel sender gauge downward to remove it.
