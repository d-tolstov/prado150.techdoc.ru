DTC    15 (4)    Throttle Motor Circuit Malfunction

для подготовки Нажмите здесь


DESCRIPTION

The throttle control motor is operated by the ECM and it opens and closes the throttle valve.
The fully open position of the throttle valve is detected by the throttle open switch mounted on the venturi.
If this DTC is stored, the ECM shuts down the power for the throttle control motor.

DTC No.
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
15 (4)
Open or short in the throttle control motor circuit.
  1. Open or short in throttle control motor circuit
  2. Throttle control motor
  3. Throttle valve
  4. Open or short in throttle open switch circuit
  5. Throttle open switch
  6. ECM
Open or short in the throttle open switch circuit.
