1. INSPECT CRANKSHAFT POSITION SENSOR

  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    1 - 2
    		Cold
    		19 to 32 Ω
    1 - 2
    		Hot
    		24 to 37 Ω

    ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
    In the chart above, the terms "Cold" and "Hot" refer to the temperature of the sensor. "Cold" means approximately -10 to 50°C (14 to 122°F). "Hot" means approximately 50 to 100°C (122 to 212°F).

    If the result is not as specified, replace the crankshaft position sensor.
