1. DISCONNECT CABLE FROM NEGATIVE BATTERY TERMINAL

ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
  1. After turning the ignition switch off, waiting time may be required before disconnecting the cable from the battery terminal. Therefore, make sure to read the disconnecting the cable from the battery terminal notice before proceeding with work (See page Нажмите здесь).
  2. When disconnecting the cable, some systems need to be initialized after the cable is reconnected (See page Нажмите здесь).



2. REMOVE FAN AND GENERATOR V BELT

  1. Remove the fan and generator V belt (See page Нажмите здесь).



3. DISCONNECT COOLER COMPRESSOR ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the 4 bolts and idle pulley bracket.




  1. Disconnect the connector.

  1. Remove the 3 bolts and disconnect the cooler compressor.

    УКАЗАНИЕ:
    It is not necessary to completely remove the cooler compressor. With the hoses connected to the compressor, hang the compressor on the vehicle body with a rope.



4. REMOVE GENERATOR ASSEMBLY



  1. Remove the terminal cap.

  1. Remove the nut and generator wire.

  1. Disconnect the generator connector.




  1. Disconnect the vacuum pump hose.

  1. Remove the union bolt to disconnect the vacuum pump oil inlet hose and remove the 2 gaskets.

  1. Detach the vacuum pump oil inlet hose from the cord clip.

  1. Disconnect the vacuum pump oil outlet hose.




  1. Remove the 2 bolts and generator.
