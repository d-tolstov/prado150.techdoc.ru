1. CHECK FOR FUEL LEAK

  1. Check that there are no fuel leaks anywhere in the fuel system after performing maintenance.

    УКАЗАНИЕ:
    When checking for fuel leaks, make sure that there is pressure in the fuel line.



2. CHECK FUEL FILTER WARNING LIGHT AND DRAIN WATER

  1. Check the fuel filter multi-information display.

    ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
    When the fuel system warning is displayed on the multi-information display, replace the fuel filter (See page Нажмите здесь) or drain the water in the fuel filter.




  1. Disconnect the level warning switch connector.

  1. Remove the 2 nuts and lift up the fuel filter.




  1. Connect a hose to the fuel filter drain plug. Place the other end of the hose into a container under the fuel filter drain plug.

  1. Loosen the fuel filter drain plug to drain water.

  1. Tighten the fuel filter drain plug by hand.

    ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
    Do not use any tools in this procedure.

  1. Install the fuel filter with the 2 nuts.

    Момент затяжки:
    18 N*m{ 184 kgf*cm , 13 ft.*lbf }

  1. Connect the level warning switch connector.
