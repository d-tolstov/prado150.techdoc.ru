УКАЗАНИЕ:
When a malfunction is not confirmed by the DTC (Diagnostic Trouble Code) check, troubleshooting should be carried out in all circuits considered to be possible causes of the problem. In many cases, by carrying out the basic engine check shown in the following flowchart, the location of the problem can be found quickly and efficiently. Therefore, using this check is essential when troubleshooting the engine.
