1. INSPECT FUEL SENDER GAUGE ASSEMBLY



  1. Check that the float moves smoothly between F and E.

  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    2 - 3
    		Float position is F (upper)
    		12 to 18 Ω
    Float position is E (lower)
    		405 to 415 Ω

    If the result is not as specified, replace the fuel sender gauge assembly.



2. INSPECT FUEL SENDER GAUGE ASSEMBLY (for Fuel Sub Tank)



  1. Check that the float moves smoothly between F and E.

  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Condition
    		Specified Condition
    1 - 2
    		Float position is F (upper)
    		12 to 18 Ω
    Float position is E (lower)
    		405 to 415 Ω

    If the result is not as specified, replace the fuel sender gauge assembly.
