1. DRAIN MANUAL TRANSMISSION OIL



  1. Remove the drain plug and gasket and then drain the manual transmission oil.




  1. Remove the filler plug and gasket.

  1. Install a new gasket and the drain plug.

    Момент затяжки:
    37 N*m{ 377 kgf*cm , 27 ft.*lbf }



2. ADD MANUAL TRANSMISSION OIL


3. INSPECT MANUAL TRANSMISSION OIL

  1. Stop the vehicle on a level surface.




  1. Check that the oil level is between 0 and 5 mm (0 and 0.196 in.) from the bottom lip of the filler plug opening.

    ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
    1. Problems may occur when the oil level is too high or low.
    2. After replacing the oil, drive the vehicle and check the oil level again.

  1. Inspect for oil leaks when the oil level is low.




  1. Install a new gasket and the filler plug.

    Момент затяжки:
    37 N*m{ 377 kgf*cm , 27 ft.*lbf }
