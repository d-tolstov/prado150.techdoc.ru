1. INSTALL OIL PUMP DRIVE GEAR

  1. Place the timing belt case on wooden blocks.

    ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
    Do not damage the timing pointer.




  1. Insert the drive and driven gears into the timing belt case with the marks facing the oil pump cover side.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Mark



2. INSTALL OIL PUMP COVER

  1. Apply adhesive to 2 or 3 threads of the screws.

    Adhesive:
    Toyota Genuine Adhesive 1344, Three Bond 1344 or equivalent

  1. Install the oil pump cover with the 8 screws.

    Момент затяжки:
    10 N*m{ 105 kgf*cm , 8 ft.*lbf }



3. INSTALL OIL PUMP RELIEF VALVE

  1. Insert the relief valve and spring into the installation hole of the timing belt case.

  1. Using a 12 mm hexagon wrench, install a new gasket and the plug.

    Момент затяжки:
    37 N*m{ 375 kgf*cm , 27 ft.*lbf }
