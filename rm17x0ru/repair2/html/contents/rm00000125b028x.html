4. REPLACE CONNECTING ROD SMALL END BUSH



  1. Using SST and a press, press out the bushing.

    SST
    09222-54011   (09222-03016, 09222-03026, 09222-03021)




  1. Using a round file, lightly file off any roughness from the small end of the connecting rod.




  1. Attach a new bush to SST with the ball of SST inside the oil hole of the bush.

    SST
    09222-54011   (09222-03021)
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Oil Hole
    *2
    		Ball




  1. Align the oil holes of the bush and connecting rod.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Oil Hole




  1. Using SST and a press, press in the bush.

    SST
    09222-54011   (09222-03016, 09222-03026, 09222-03021)
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Bush




  1. Using a pin hole grinder, hone the bush to obtain the clearance between the bush and piston pin.



  1. Check the piston pin fit at normal room temperature.




    1. Coat the piston pin with engine oil, and push it into the connecting rod with your thumb.
