1. DRAIN BRAKE FLUID FROM CLUTCH LINE

  1. Remove the front propeller shaft (See page Нажмите здесь).

  1. Using a union nut wrench, disconnect the tube.

  1. Remove the 2 bolts and clutch release cylinder.
