1. REMOVE TIMING BELT

  1. Remove the timing belt (See page Нажмите здесь).



2. REMOVE DIESEL THROTTLE BODY

  1. Remove the diesel throttle body (See page Нажмите здесь).



3. REMOVE INJECTION PIPE SET



  1. Using a union nut wrench, loosen the 8 union nuts of the 4 injection pipes.
    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Union Nut Wrench
    *a
    		for Injection Nozzle Side
    *b
    		for Injection Pump Side

  1. Remove the 2 nuts, 2 upper pipe clamps and 4 injection pipes with 2 lower pipe clamps.



4. REMOVE INJECTION PUMP DRIVE PULLEY



  1. Using SST, remove the pulley nut.

    SST
    09213-14010   (91651-60865)
    09330-00021  




  1. Using SST, remove the drive pulley.

    SST
    09950-50013   (09951-05010, 09952-05010, 09953-05010, 09954-05021)



5. REMOVE INJECTION PUMP ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the 5 connectors and detach the wire harness clamp.

  1. Disconnect the 3 fuel hoses.




  1. Remove the 3 bolts and injection pump stay.

  1. Remove the 2 nuts and injection pump.
