1. REMOVE TIMING BELT

  1. Remove the timing belt (See page Нажмите здесь).



2. REMOVE CRANKSHAFT TIMING PULLEY



  1. Using a screwdriver, remove the crankshaft timing pulley.

    SST
    09950-50013   (09951-05010, 09952-05010, 09953-05010, 09953-05020, 09954-05010)

    УКАЗАНИЕ:
    If the timing pulley cannot be removed by using a screwdriver, remove it with SST.



3. REMOVE FRONT CRANKSHAFT OIL SEAL



  1. Using a knife, cut off the lip of the oil seal.
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		Cut Position

  1. Using a screwdriver, pry out the oil seal.

    ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
    Do not damage the crankshaft.

    ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
    Tape the screwdriver before use.
