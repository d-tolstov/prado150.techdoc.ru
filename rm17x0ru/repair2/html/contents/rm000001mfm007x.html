DTC    14 (4)    Timing Control System Malfunction

для подготовки Нажмите здесь


DESCRIPTION

The ECM controls the injection timing by actuating the timing control valve. The timing control valve is mounted on the injection pump and controls the internal fuel pressure of the pump through duty control.
The ECM detects the injection advance angle based on TDC and NE signals.

DTC No.
DTC Detection Condition
Trouble Area
14 (4)
During and after warming up the engine, the actual injection timing is different from the target value that the ECM calculated for several seconds.
  1. Open or short in timing control valve circuit
  2. Timing control valve
  3. Fuel filter element assembly (clogging)
  4. Fuel (frozen, air present)
  5. Injection pump assembly (internal pressure and timing control valve)
  6. ECM
