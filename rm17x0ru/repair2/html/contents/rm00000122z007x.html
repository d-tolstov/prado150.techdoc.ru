1. REMOVE RESONATOR WITH AIR CLEANER CAP SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Disconnect the sensor connector.

  1. Detach the wire harness clamp.

  1. Loosen the hose clamp and remove the resonator with air cleaner cap.




  1. Detach the 4 hook clamps, and then remove the air cleaner cap and resonator with air cleaner cap.



2. REMOVE INTAKE AIR CONNECTOR SUB-ASSEMBLY



  1. Loosen the hose clamp and remove the 2 bolts and intake pipe.



3. REMOVE CYLINDER HEAD COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove the 9 bolts, nut, cylinder head cover and gasket.



4. SET NO. 1 CYLINDER TO TDC/COMPRESSION



  1. Turn the crankshaft pulley and align its groove with the timing pointer.

  1. Check that the valve lifters for the No. 1 cylinder are loose and the valve lifters for the No. 4 cylinder are tight.
    If not, turn the crankshaft 1 revolution (360°) and align the mark as above.

6. ADJUST VALVE CLEARANCE



  1. Remove the adjusting shim.

    1. Turn the crankshaft so that the cam lobe of the camshaft on the valve being adjusted points upward.

    2. Using SST, press down the valve lifter.

      SST
      09248-64011  

    3. Position the notch of the valve lifter so that it faces the exhaust manifold side.




    4. Remove the adjusting shim with a screwdriver and magnet hand.




  1. Determine the replacement adjusting shim size according to the formula and charts below.

    1. Using a micrometer, measure the thickness of the removed shim.

    2. Calculate the thickness of a new shim so that the valve clearance comes within the specified value.
      T = Thickness of removed shim
      A = Measured valve clearance
      N = Thickness of new shim
      Intake
      		N = T + (A - 0.25 mm (0.00984 in.))
      Exhaust
      		N = T + (A - 0.45 mm (0.0177 in.))



    3. Select a new shim with a thickness as close as possible to the calculated value.

      УКАЗАНИЕ:
      Shims are available in 17 sizes in increments of 0.05 mm (0.00197 in.), from 2.50 mm (0.0984 in.) to 3.30 mm (0.130 in.).

7. INSTALL CYLINDER HEAD COVER SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Remove any old packing (FIPG material).




  1. Apply seal packing to the cylinder head as shown in the illustration.

    Seal packing:
    Toyota Genuine Seal Packing Black, Three Bond 1207B or equivalent

    Text in Illustration 
    *1
    		Seal Packing

  1. Install the gasket to the cylinder head cover.

  1. Install the cylinder head cover with the 9 bolts and nut. Uniformly tighten the bolts and nut in several steps.

    Момент затяжки:
    12 N*m{ 122 kgf*cm , 9 ft.*lbf }



8. INSTALL INTAKE AIR CONNECTOR SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Install the intake pipe with the 2 bolts.

    Момент затяжки:
    18 N*m{ 184 kgf*cm , 13 ft.*lbf }

  1. Tighten the intake pipe clamp.

    Момент затяжки:
    6.0 N*m{ 61 kgf*cm , 53 in.*lbf }



9. INSTALL RESONATOR WITH AIR CLEANER CAP SUB-ASSEMBLY

  1. Вставьте петли крышки воздушного фильтра и шланг в корпус воздушного фильтра, а затем закрепите 4 откидных защелки.

  1. Установите крышку воздушного фильтра и закрепите ее зажимом.

    Момент затяжки:
    5,0 N*m{ 51 kgf*cm , 44 in.*lbf }

  1. Закрепите зажим жгута проводов.

  1. Подсоедините 2 зажима и разъем.



10. INSPECT ENGINE IDLE SPEED

  1. Warm up the engine.

  1. When using the intelligent tester:

    1. Connect the intelligent tester to the DLC3.

      Idle speed:
      720 to 820 rpm

      ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
      1. Turn all the electrical systems and the A/C off.
      2. When checking the idling speed, the shift lever should be in neutral.

      УКАЗАНИЕ:
      Refer to the intelligent tester operator's manual for further details.




  1. When not using an intelligent tester:

    1. Using SST, connect the tachometer test probe to terminal 9 (TAC) of the DLC3.

    2. SST
      09843-18030  
      Check the idle speed.

      Standard idle speed:
      720 to 820 rpm

      Text in Illustration 
      *a
      		Front View of DLC3

      ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
      1. Turn all the electrical systems and the A/C off.
      2. When checking the idling speed, the shift lever should be in neutral.
      3. Confirm the terminal number before connecting them. Connecting the wrong terminal can be damage the engine.



11. INSPECT MAXIMUM ENGINE SPEED

  1. Start the engine.

  1. Fully depress the accelerator pedal.

  1. Check the maximum speed.

    Maximum engine speed:
    4850 to 4950 rpm
