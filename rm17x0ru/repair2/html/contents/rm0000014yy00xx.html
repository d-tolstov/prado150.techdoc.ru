1. INSPECT LEVEL WARNING SWITCH



  1. Measure the resistance according to the value(s) in the table below.

    Standard Resistance:
    Tester Connection
    		Switch Condition
    		Specified Condition
    1 - 2
    		On
    		7.4 to 9.5 Ω
    1 - 2
    		Off
    		10 kΩ or higher

    If the result is not as specified, replace the level warning switch.
