3. INSTALL NO. 2 TRANSMISSION CLUTCH HUB



  1. Apply a light coat of gear oil to the sleeve and hub.

  1. Install the No. 2 clutch hub to the clutch hub sleeve.
    Text in Illustration 
    *a
    		4th Gear Side
    *b
    		3rd Gear Side

  1. Install one of the balls to each shifting key.

  1. Install one of the springs to each shifting key.

  1. Install the shifting keys with balls and springs to the No. 2 clutch hub.

    УКАЗАНИЕ:
    Make sure that the No. 2 clutch hub faces the correct direction as shown in the illustration.

    ПРИМЕЧАНИЕ:
    Be careful to prevent the balls from flying off.

19. INSPECT 6TH GEAR THRUST CLEARANCE



  1. Using a dial indicator, measure the 6th gear thrust clearance.

    Standard clearance:
    0.20 to 0.49 mm (0.00788 to 0.0193 in.)

    If the clearance is outside the specification, replace the gear, spacer or shaft.

    УКАЗАНИЕ:
    Replace the part or parts determined to be the most likely cause of the problem.



20. INSPECT 3RD GEAR THRUST CLEARANCE



  1. Using a feeler gauge, measure the 3rd gear thrust clearance.

    Standard clearance:
    0.09 to 0.52 mm (0.00355 to 0.0205 in.)

    If the clearance is outside the specification, replace the gear, thrust washer, clutch hub or shaft.

    УКАЗАНИЕ:
    Replace the part or parts determined to be the most likely cause of the problem.
